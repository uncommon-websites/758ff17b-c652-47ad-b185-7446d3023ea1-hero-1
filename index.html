Creative Australia is the Australian Government’s principal arts investment and advisory body. We deliver industry growth and cultural impact via strategic grants, mentoring, and international market development.

With 50 years of experience, we support Australian creative professionals through sustainable financial investment, building global cultural influence and fostering First Nations self-determination.

Investing in the creative life of all Australians.
